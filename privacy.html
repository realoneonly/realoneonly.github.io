Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Short version: This site does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. We don't track you. We don't have accounts. We're here to educate, not exploit.

1. What We Collect

This website currently collects no personal information. There are no sign-up forms, no accounts, no login, and no cookies set by us. If you use the quiz feature, your answers are processed entirely in your browser and never sent to any server.

2. Third-Party Services

This site uses Google Fonts (loaded from Google's CDN) to display typography. Google may log your IP address when fonts are loaded. This is standard behavior for CDN-hosted fonts. We have no control over Google's data practices. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com for details.

If this site is hosted on GitHub Pages, GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, browser types) as part of their infrastructure. See GitHub's Privacy Policy at docs.github.com/en/site-policy for details.

3. Analytics

We currently run no analytics or tracking software. No cookies. No fingerprinting. No third-party ad networks. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and users will be informed.

4. Children's Privacy

This site is designed for young people ages 11 and up. Because we collect no personal data at all, we are compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by design. We encourage parents and educators to review this policy.

5. Your Rights

Since we collect no data, there is nothing to request, delete, or export. You have complete privacy by default.

6. Contact

7. Changes

If our practices change (e.g., we add a newsletter or community feature), we will update this policy and add a clear notice on the homepage before any data collection begins.
