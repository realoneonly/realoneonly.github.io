Question 01 / 04
If you put $100 in a savings account earning 0.01% interest, and inflation is 3% ‚Äî what happened to your money?
Correct. Inflation means prices rise ‚Äî so $100 buys less next year than this year. If your money earns less than inflation, you're losing purchasing power even as the number stays the same. This is why saving alone isn't enough.
Question 02 / 04
Which is worth more: $1,000 given to you today, or $1,000 given to you in 5 years?
Today. This is "time value of money" ‚Äî $1,000 today can be invested and grow. In 5 years at 7% return, today's $1,000 becomes ~$1,400. Money now is always worth more than money later.
Question 03 / 04
A Roblox developer creates a game that earns 1,000 Robux/day while they sleep. This is an example of:
Passive income ‚Äî value created once that keeps earning. This is the difference between trading time for money vs. building something that earns independently. Most wealthy people have multiple passive income streams.
Question 04 / 04
Someone scrolling social media for 3 hours sees 50 ads. Who is making money in this situation?
The platform sells your attention to advertisers. They earn billions. You earn nothing. Understanding this doesn't mean you quit social media ‚Äî it means you start thinking about being a creator on it, not just a consumer.
